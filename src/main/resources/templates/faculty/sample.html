Week
Course Contents

1. Introduction:

    • 1.1. Classification of motor vehicles
    • 1.2. Transmission of motion to wheel

2. Pneumatic Tires and Wheels:

    • 2.1. Classification of motor vehicles
    • 2.2. Classification of motor vehicle
    • 2.3. Roiling resistance
    • 2.4. Slip angle and cornering moment

3. Suspension Systems:

    • 3.1. Springs and shock absorbers
    • 3.2. Suspension systems classification
    • 3.3. Configuration and roll centers of dependent and independent

Mid Exam

4. Power Train:

    • 4.1 Clutch
    • 4.2 Sliding mesh and synchromesh gear box
    • 4.3 Differential gearbox and transfer case
    • 4.4 Planetary gearbox
    • 4.5 Automatic transmission

5. Road Performance of Motor Vehicles::

    • 5.1 Resistance force on motor vehicle
    • 5.2 Tractive force diagram of motor vehicle
    • 5.3 Steady motion performance
    • 5.4 Acceleration performance

6. Braking system:

    • 6.1 Hydraulic braking system with and without booster.
    • 6.2 Braking moments for shoe and disc brakes.
    • 6.3 Antilock braking system.
    • 6.4 Distance travelled during braking.

Final Exam
